Project 1: Calculator: Because Who Needs Innovation Anyway?

Behold, my 'innovative' calculator app that's just like Google Calculator, because, you know, why reinvent the wheel? I've also taken the revolutionary step of applying the '60-30-10 rule' for visual appeal, because apparently, 59-31-10 is just too wild. Whether you're a math genius or just someone who can't be bothered to do mental math, my app is here to make your life slightly less miserable. Try it out, and maybe it won't ruin your day!
